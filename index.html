Hi! Welcome home.


My name is Pharo Hutsteiner. I am a Data Analyst with experience in using Excel, Google Sheets, SQL and Tableau to extract actionable insights from real-world data. I complement this regularly with my programming experience in languages such as C, Python, HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

My professional journey into the world of analytics has been an unconventional one. I studied History and Politics at the University of Warwick, graduating in 2023 with a high 2.1 degree. My pivot towards tech was spurred on by unexpectedly discovering Harvard University's introductory course on Computer Science: CS50x. I quickly became obsessed with my newfound ability to build things - from low-level programmes to web applications (see on Github how I built this space)!

My approach to data analysis is very much informed by this same desire to create something real and tangible - in this case: insights for positive social change. I undertook 16 weeks of training at The Curious Academy (Reading), supplemented with self-paced learning via DataCamp, which culminated in a final project focussing on the current Car Driving Test shortage in Great Britain. I am fortunate to operate in an industry where I feel continually challenged to learn new things every day. Browse through the pages on this site for a comprehensive snapshot of what I'm up to: from articles on exploratory data analysis to coding projects.
